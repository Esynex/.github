## What is Esynex?

**Esynex** is a unified messaging platform that helps businesses manage customer conversations across multiple channels — WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, Telegram, Instagram, TikTok, and Email — from a single dashboard.

Train an AI assistant to handle routine questions automatically, while your team steps in when a personal touch is needed. Built with Sri Lankan businesses in mind, with support for **Sinhala** and **English**.

> *Unified · AI-Driven · Managed*

## Who is it for?

Esynex is designed for **small and medium businesses** that receive a high volume of customer messages and want faster, more consistent service — without juggling multiple apps.

| Audience | Example |
|----------|---------|
| Retail shops | Product inquiries, stock checks, order updates |
| Tuition centers | Class info, fees, enrollment questions |
| Restaurants | Menu, reservations, delivery status |
| Creators & brands | Fan messages, collab requests, product DMs |

## Use Cases

### 1. One inbox for every channel
Customers message you on WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram, and more. Instead of switching between apps, your team sees and replies to everything in one place.

### 2. AI handles the repetitive questions
Train your assistant to answer FAQs — pricing, hours, class schedules, menu items — in Sinhala, English, or both. Free up your team for conversations that actually need a human.

### 3. Never miss what matters
Get notified when a message needs personal attention — a complaint, a high-intent buyer, or something the AI can't handle confidently.

### 4. Email alongside social
Manage business email from the same platform — contacts, templates, and outbound messages without leaving the dashboard.

### 5. Stay on top of your business
View activity at a glance, track how your channels are performing, and broadcast announcements across connected platforms when you have news to share.

### 6. Onboard in minutes
Sign up, set up your business profile, connect your channels, and start managing conversations — no technical expertise required.
